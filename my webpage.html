About Me

My Expertise

System Engineering: I have extensive experience in designing, implementing, and managing complex systems. My work involves ensuring that all components of a system work together seamlessly, optimizing performance and reliability.

Network Administration: With a deep understanding of network protocols and infrastructure, I excel in setting up and maintaining secure and efficient networks. My role often includes troubleshooting network issues, configuring hardware, and ensuring high availability.

Web Development: I am proficient in both front-end and back-end web development. Using technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks such as React and Django, I build responsive and user-friendly web applications. My expertise in Python further enhances my ability to develop dynamic and scalable web solutions.

Technical Sales: As a former technical sales representative, I have a solid background in selling computers, peripherals, and home broadband devices. This experience has equipped me with valuable skills in understanding customer needs, providing tailored solutions, and effectively communicating technical information to non-technical audiences.
